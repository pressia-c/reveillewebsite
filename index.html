AI & Language Intelligence

Understanding Our AI

Natural Language Processing analyzes your word choices, sentence patterns, and emotional vocabulary to reveal insights about your inner state. This is sophisticated AI that works entirely through your language.

What we detect: Hesitation markers, certainty indicators, emotional shifts, recurring themes, and temporal focus. All observations come from your writing patterns.

Important: The system does not replace therapy or human meaning-making. It observes structure and recurrence.

Built for Individuals and Therapists

Individuals

  • Personal Archive Private longitudinal writing space.
  • Insight Web Visual map of recurring themes.
  • Selective Sharing Share specific entries with a therapist.
  • Preventative Pattern Awareness Detect trends before crisis.
  • Data Ownership Full export and archive control.

Therapists

  • Enterprise Dashboard Manage multiple client profiles within one structured interface.
  • Client Profile Vault Each client has a dedicated archive.
  • Shared Vault Architecture Client-shared entries appear in both accounts. Synchronized, but accounts remain separate.
  • Private Clinical Notes Therapist session notes remain invisible unless marked shared.
  • Optional Shared Notes Therapist can make notes visible to client.
  • Pattern Contribution System Therapist annotations contribute to the client's Insight Web.
  • Unified Longitudinal View Track thematic development across sessions.

Account Structure

Individual and Therapist accounts are fully separate systems. Sharing occurs only at the specific file level. No automatic merging of accounts. Explicit consent-based sharing only.
